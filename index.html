Exterior Car Wash

Washing your car regularly is a big part of maintaining your vehicle's paint and keeping your car's body clean and corrosion-free. Over time, the contaminants and pollutants that are present in the air slowly build up on your vehicle, eating through the clear coat and degrading your car's paint. Our mobile car wash auto detailing service offers a superior exterior clean, removing dirt and giving your vehicle a beautiful shine, all while using the highest quality cleaning and car wash products on the market.

Wax

If your car is left outside after our mobile car wash, the sun, rain and other elements will take their toll on it. Protect your investment with a car wax mobile auto detailing service that repels water and dirt, leaving your vehicle looking clean. Beyond just protecting your car from the elements, a car wax also gives it a beautiful shine, leaving it looking clean without swirl marks. We use ceramic wax so you know you are getting the absolute best.

Ceramic Coating

Ceramic coatings (also known as ceramic paint protection or clear coat) are among the most innovative and effective products used to create lustrous, paint-deepening shine and protection in the auto detailing industry. The ceramic coating is applied to the paint of the car, truck, or van to provide an extra layer of paint protection film against the elements and prevent the need for future paint correction. And while ceramic coatings are a more expensive option than traditional wax or paint sealant, they are incredibly durable. Usually lasting at least 3 to 5 years or more, ceramic coatings give a car that beautiful clean and "waxed" look without the regular maintenance.

Interior Detailing

When it comes to car detailing, the overall appearance of your vehicle's interior is just as important as its exterior. Even the most beautiful car can look neglected if the interior is not properly maintained, and a car that is in poor condition inside is a major turnoff. Vehicles are often exposed to harsh sunlight, and the interior panels, floor mats, and seating regularly come into contact with dirt, grime and oils. We take interior car detail to the next level with our mobile detailing, giving all components a deep cleaning, vacuuming loose dirt and debris and removing any bad odors.

Headlight Restoration

Headlight Restoration is a procedure that restores the clarity of the lens on your headlights. Despite their importance, they are often overlooked during the auto detailing and cleaning process, but there are many benefits to restoring this part of your car. With our mobile car detailing headlight restoration, we give your headlights a preliminary restoration & protection, leaving your headlights looking perfect.

My local car wash offers detailing services at half the price, what's the difference?

We pride ourselves on offering exceptional customer service for the past 3 years. We know your time is important. Enjoy the freedom to continue your day without having to drive and wait around the car wash. Also the term ‘Detailing’ has been widly misused in the industry. A true detail consists of meticulous deep cleaning & swirl free polishing. Inexperienced detailers can run a hi-speed rotary machine over your paint incorrectly and burn through the clear coat of paint causing extreme damage or swirl marks. Trust the professionals. Make sure you ask questions before allowing anyone to detail your car!

Will steam cleaning my carpets & seats remove all stains?

Unfortunately no. While pre-treating and steam extraction (part of our service) is the most effective cleaning procedure available, some spills (especially any with reddish dyes), can actually damage and dye carpet and upholstery fibers. Once this occurs, these fabrics can be cleaned but color change is permanent unless material is replaced or re-dyed.

The dealership added a paint sealant and said my car never has to be waxed again, is this true?

No. This is truly a play on words, meaning that ‘sealant‘ still needs to be re-applied every three to six months. Hence the statement “never wax again” simply means sealant rather than wax, must be applied again.

Why should I detail my car?

Waxing has always made cars extra shiny, but modern paint jobs and wax formulations have improved a lot in recent years. Paint used to be just that, paint. A new car got a layer of primer and a few coats of colored lacquer, and that was it. Wax not only gave the paint a good gloss, it was also the only line of defense against scratches. Beginning in the 1980s, manufacturers started adding a layer of clear coat, which seals the paint and adds to the shine of the car. The clear coat also takes the environmental abuse. Things like ultraviolet light, ozone, exhaust, salt, dirt, rain, bug guts, and bird poop build up tiny scratches and oxidation on the clear coat’s surface. As the paint ages, that damage causes the surface to get hazy and the shine to subside, but there’s generally no damage to the color layer below. Not waxing will leave your car looking dull and the clear coat vulnerable to accelerated wear. Waxing provides a sacrificial layer on top of the clear coat. We recommend getting your vehicles detailed once a year.
